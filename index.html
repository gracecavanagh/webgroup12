Team - Pocket 3D

Ideal

A web-based solution that is able to easily be manipulated and angled in order to easily see the environment's surroundings - ideally something they can use on a portable device to look at while on-site.

Reality

Currently there are no easy ways to visualize an environment while walking on-site that allows users to see - rather than just read - factors like elevation, slope, and surrounding objects. It takes time to plan complex work in person all while on-foot and may require more personnel to be involved in the task.

Consequences

Maitenence planning can be time consuming and therefore costly if more manhours are needed to complete any work. It can be easy to overlook surrounding hazards while in the field, increasing risk.

Proposal

Test and explore different software that will allow us to create a detailed, accurate 3D model of our environment that can be utilized on a mobile device.
